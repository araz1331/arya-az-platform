CEO Recommendation for PH Launch
The "Visual Hook": For the terminal window, use a font like Courier New or JetBrains Mono to give it that "Developer Security" vibe.

The Color Palette: Keep it dark (Slate-900) with Neon Green accents. This psychologically triggers "Security & Health" (perfect for AI).
